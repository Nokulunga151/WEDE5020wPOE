Our History

Nina's Café was founded in 2013 by Nina, a coffee admirer who travelled the world enjoying the best brews and pastries. Nina wanted to share those flavours and experiences with people in her hometown, which is when she came up with the idea to open her own café.


Our Timeline

2013 - Nina's Café founded as a small family-owned coffee shop in Johannesburg, focusing on freshly brewed coffee and homemade pastries.

2015 - Expanded the menu to include specialty drinks (lattes, cappuccinos, mochas) and added a small dessert section.

2017 - Introduced community events such as poetry nights and live acoustic music, making the café a local cultural hub.

2019 - Opened a second branch in Cape Town to serve a growing customer base.

2020 - During the COVID-19 pandemic, launched online ordering and takeaway services to adapt to customer needs.

2022 - Started sourcing coffee beans directly from fair-trade farmers in Africa to support sustainability.

2024 - Rebranded with a fresh logo, new colour palette, and website development plans (ninascafe.com).

2025 - Launch of a new e-commerce website with online ordering, blog, and “Build Your Own Latte” feature.
